## Additional Practice Ideas

Try writing programs that:

- Print numbers from 1 to 100.
- Print numbers from 100 to 1.
- Print only even numbers.
- Print only odd numbers.
- Print multiples of 5.
- Print multiples of 10.
- Count backwards.
- Skip numbers by 3.
- Print the first 20 natural numbers.
- Print your favorite number 10 times.

## Interview Preparation

Be able to answer:

- What is a `for` loop?
- When should a `for` loop be used?
- Explain Initialization, Condition, and Update.
- What happens if the condition is always `true`?
- Difference between `for` and `while`.
- Difference between `i++` and `i += 2`.
- What is an infinite loop?

---

## Common Mistakes

- Forgetting to update the counter.
- Using the wrong comparison operator.
- Updating the counter in the wrong direction.
- Off-by-one errors.
- Creating an unintended infinite loop.
